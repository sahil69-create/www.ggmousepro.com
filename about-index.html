Our Story
From a passion project to the leading mobile gaming enhancement tool, discover the journey of GG Mouse Pro.

GG Mouse Pro is a simple yet powerful tool designed to give mobile gamers a PC-like experience using mouse and keyboard controls – without the need for complex setups or rooting.

It all started in 2024 when we noticed how limited touch controls can be, especially in fast-paced or competitive games. So, we built an easy-to-use solution that works for everyone – no extra hardware or PC required.

Today, thousands of gamers rely on GG Mouse Pro for smoother gameplay and better control. We manually deliver premium codes within 24 hours and continuously update the app based on user feedback to improve performance and compatibility.
